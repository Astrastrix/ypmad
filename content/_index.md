# Our Story
MAD is a life-changing program for young people aged 12-18 battling everyday challenges. We create a tight-knit family atmosphere where they can fearlessly share their struggles. What sets us apart? Our secret weapon: peer support. Within our program, these remarkable young individuals find answers and inspiration from others who've walked a similar path. Together, they conquer and thrive!
